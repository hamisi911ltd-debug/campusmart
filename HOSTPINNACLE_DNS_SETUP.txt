═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  ADD DNS RECORDS IN HOSTPINNACLE cPANEL
  Connect campusmart.co.ke to Firebase
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Your domain is registered with HostPinnacle, so you'll add DNS
records in their cPanel.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 1: GET DNS RECORDS FROM FIREBASE (2 MINUTES)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to Firebase Console:
   https://console.firebase.google.com/project/campusmart-7d3d4/hosting

2. Click "Add custom domain"

3. Enter: campusmart.co.ke

4. Click "Continue"

5. Firebase will show DNS records like:

   A Records:
   • 151.101.1.195
   • 151.101.65.195
   
   TXT Record:
   • (verification code)

6. Keep this page open - you'll need these values!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 2: LOGIN TO HOSTPINNACLE cPANEL (1 MINUTE)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to: https://hostpinnacle.co.ke/cpanel
   Or: https://campusmart.co.ke:2083

2. Login with your HostPinnacle credentials:
   • Username: (your HostPinnacle username)
   • Password: (your HostPinnacle password)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 3: OPEN ZONE EDITOR (1 MINUTE)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

In cPanel:

1. Scroll down to "Domains" section

2. Click "Zone Editor"
   (or "Advanced DNS Zone Editor")

3. Find "campusmart.co.ke" in the list

4. Click "Manage" or "Edit" next to it

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 4: DELETE OLD A RECORDS (2 MINUTES)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IMPORTANT: Remove existing A records first!

1. Look for existing A records for campusmart.co.ke

2. You'll see records pointing to HostPinnacle IP

3. Delete these old A records:
   • Click "Delete" or trash icon next to each A record
   • Confirm deletion

4. Keep other records (MX, TXT, etc.) - only delete A records!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 5: ADD NEW A RECORDS FOR FIREBASE (3 MINUTES)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Now add Firebase A records:

First A Record:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Click "Add Record" or "+ A Record"

2. Fill in:
   • Name: @ (or leave blank, or campusmart.co.ke)
   • Type: A
   • Address/Value: 151.101.1.195
   • TTL: 14400 (or leave default)

3. Click "Add Record" or "Save"

Second A Record:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Click "Add Record" again

2. Fill in:
   • Name: @ (or leave blank, or campusmart.co.ke)
   • Type: A
   • Address/Value: 151.101.65.195
   • TTL: 14400 (or leave default)

3. Click "Add Record" or "Save"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 6: ADD TXT RECORD FOR VERIFICATION (2 MINUTES)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Click "Add Record" or "+ TXT Record"

2. Fill in:
   • Name: @ (or leave blank)
   • Type: TXT
   • TXT Data/Value: (paste the verification code from Firebase)
   • TTL: 14400 (or leave default)

3. Click "Add Record" or "Save"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 7: VERIFY IN FIREBASE (1 MINUTE)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go back to Firebase Console

2. Click "Verify" button

3. Firebase will check the DNS records

4. If successful, you'll see "Domain verified" ✅

5. If not, wait 5-10 minutes and try again

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  STEP 8: WAIT FOR SSL CERTIFICATE (15 MINS - 24 HOURS)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

After verification:

1. Firebase will provision SSL certificate

2. This usually takes 15 minutes to 24 hours

3. You'll get email when ready

4. Status will show "Connected" in Firebase Console

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  TROUBLESHOOTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Problem: Can't find Zone Editor
Solution:
  • Try searching "DNS" in cPanel search bar
  • Or look for "Advanced DNS Zone Editor"
  • Or contact HostPinnacle support

Problem: Can't delete old A records
Solution:
  • Some records might be protected
  • Contact HostPinnacle support to remove them
  • They can do it for you

Problem: "Domain verification failed"
Solution:
  • Wait 10-15 minutes after adding records
  • Check TXT record is exactly as Firebase shows
  • Try verification again

Problem: Still showing 404 after 24 hours
Solution:
  • Check A records are correct (151.101.1.195 and 151.101.65.195)
  • Make sure old A records are deleted
  • Clear browser cache
  • Try incognito/private browsing
